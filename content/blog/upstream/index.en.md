Hey, it's him again, and another year has passed. Despite being a flamboyant figure in the arena for many years, having tasted all delicacies and crossed paths with all kinds of people, this year felt exceptionally long and wide to him

Wandering the internet looking for a turning point in life, hesitating between light work with high pay versus light work with low pay, he found Upstream. This is probably the first Chinese film without martial arts elements he has watched. Movies and music are always the rising and falling notes capturing the shifting tides of time. He loves the quote: "I used to think the house was the most important thing, but now I know it is actually our family"

Having a woman willing to stand by him through difficult times is worth more than all the glittering things in the world

Do not ask why he is so obsessed with Her 4.5-span, juicy bust. When his palm is filled, his body immediately secretes Oxytocin: a hormone of happiness. Though it cannot fill his stomach or turn the market from red to green, Oxytocin fills him with faith in a prosperous future, helping him weather the stormy days

Since he is already at the bottom, he wishes everyone a New Year 2025 where every step is a step upward
