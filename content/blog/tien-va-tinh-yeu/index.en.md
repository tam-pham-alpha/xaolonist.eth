Hey, it's him again, and according to Einstein's equations, the world has about 11 dimensions. What is the key for him to access these new dimensions?

Ever since finding love, Binz's music sounds incredibly deep: whether one likes it or not, that very aura is what makes each person distinct

He watched Materialists: those who have money always talk about value, while those who don't have much money talk about love

If forced to choose between love and money, he would choose love. But if forced to choose between a lot of love and a lot of money, he still does not have an answer

To him, money, love, history, physics, philosophy, and the dark arts are the essential keys to accessing new dimensions
