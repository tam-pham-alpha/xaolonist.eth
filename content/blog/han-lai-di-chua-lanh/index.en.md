Hey, it's him again: he goes to heal again. However many scars lie on his thick face, his heart has been cut just as deeply

He recalls around 2019 wandering in Lac Duong, Langbiang, where he stumbled upon a pizza shop. Long tables made of scrap wood, yet neat and orderly. The oven was built by hand: square in some places, round in others: yet the hearth was always warm. He is drawn to such things

The owner was a foreigner named James, with a Vietnamese wife. He had a young daughter: blonde and extremely adorable. Her daughter must be a teenager by now. He always wished to have a daughter like that. He thought: in the future, he would take his daughter shopping to amaze the world

This time, he stayed one night at Hana Homestay. The specialty of Mang Den is forest rain: incredibly exhausting

Nearby, something caught his eye. Long, neat wooden tables, giving him a warm and close feeling. He ordered pizza, French fries, wine, and beer. He asked around and learned that Uncle Tuan was a friend and also a student of Uncle James. How is the earth so round? he thought

His life has seen many pizzas: Uncle Tuan's pizza was second only to Pizza 4P's. He ate a lot that day
