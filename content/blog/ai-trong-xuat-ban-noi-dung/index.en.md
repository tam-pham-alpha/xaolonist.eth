Hey, it's him again, and lately many people are worried that AI will steal writers' jobs or replace humans entirely in content creation. But to him, AI has never been a scary replacement. It is like a strange companion, a powerful partner helping him realize his thoughts faster than ever.

Many use AI to write articles from A to Z, entrusting the very soul of the piece to a machine. He finds that approach quite dull. An article without real-life experience, without pain, joy, or quiet moments of observing life is just a hollow sequence of words. AI can write correct grammar and use the most beautiful vocabulary, but it does not know the feeling of peace when watching Her sit next to him, nor the harshness of a trade going against the trend.

The "soul", or the inner awareness and the truth of the writing, must always originate from the human.

But once the soul is there, AI helps him handle the "body."

He only needs to tell AI a raw idea, a reflection that just flashed in his mind. AI will then help him restructure the article into standardized Markdown, translate it smoothly into English, automatically find and crop cover images to perfect ratios, and push the code straight to GitHub for Cloudflare Pages to deploy to the internet.

This combination gives him unprecedented flexibility and freedom:
1.  **Humans hold the guiding role (The Soul)**: Focusing entirely on experiencing life, reflecting, and uncovering the truth.
2.  **AI holds the executing role (The Body)**: Handling technical processing, formatting Markdown, optimizing SEO, translating, and managing automated scripts.

Thanks to AI, the gap between an idea in his mind and a complete post on the blog is now measured in minutes. He no longer has to worry about repetitive mouse clicks or complex server configurations.

The magic of technology has now merged with the simplicity of static text files. He is still The Fool walking on his own path, but in his hands, he now holds powerful keys to access new dimensions of creativity.
