Hey, it's him again. She is a girl hard to please, because life has so many things that tease.

She is a girl hard to please<br/>
because life has so many things that tease<br/>
she doesn't care for luxury brands<br/>
and never tells lies or makes demands

She calls him an artist<br/>
because he likes many things<br/>
and sometimes he has tricks and wings<br/>
he studies history, dragons, and past kings<br/>
and about adventures that time brings

She is a psychologist<br/>
And he is a xaolonist

She has many questions<br/>
giving him long questionnaires<br/>
his head is spinning<br/>
because the problems are not fair

She doesn't care to be on top<br/>
nor does she care to be trendy<br/>
she can just be herself: nerdy and crazy<br/>
he loves Den while she likes BinZ<br/>
He doesn't give a fuck<br/>
in the end she is still amazing

Remembering the first day drinking beer with her on the street<br/>
they talked about baked pies and things sweet<br/>
Airbnb breakfast in bed<br/>
their story was sweet enough without sugar instead

People call him a playboy<br/>
not just playing on home turf<br/>
his footprints in the west and east surf<br/>
his collection full of checklists<br/>
and his life has many hypotheses

That's Binz and this is xaolonist<br/>
bad boy he doesn't have to act<br/>
good boy he doesn't have to play<br/>
He doesn't give a fuck<br/>
about what tomorrow might say<br/>
all he looks forward to<br/>
is living fully in the present day
