Hey, it's him again, @anh4gs: he has an intense passion for cats, having been an expert in cat raising with over 10 years of research and at least 6 years of skin in the game

Countless cats have helplessly rejoiced under the caresses of his divine hand: British Shorthairs, Longhairs, Scottish Folds, tabbies, and Tuxedos. His beloved Mickey was no exception

And yet, he recently searched through ancient archives: in a chapter titled Negavellus, within the book The Dark Magic, a mythical creature was mentioned named Pussybreviora auricularis, colloquially known as the short-eared cat

This breed carries immense power. People believe that by looking deep into its bright red nose, mortals will glimpse their ups and downs across many lifetimes, and hear the cosmic secrets the universe never wished to disclose

He once took pride in the feline knowledge he accumulated, yet he must bow his head and admit his own shallowness regarding this mystical creature. Is it true that only when a person becomes humble like an empty cup, the stream of knowledge can flow in abundantly?
