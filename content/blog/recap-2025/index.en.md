Hey, it's him again. 2025 was an incredibly interesting year for him, and he always jokes that it provided him with the necessary ingredients to write the next chapters of his life, filled with fragrance and color

The fire of wisdom burned away all the clutter and entanglements in his life, leaving within him an indescribable emptiness, and accidentally granting him a freedom he had never known before

He knows this is the time to start anew, to fill that space with more selective things. Or even if he deliberately leaves it empty, the fresh breeze of 2026 will still fill it naturally
