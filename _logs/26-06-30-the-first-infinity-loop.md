# The First Infinity Loop

## Context

Create a bilingual bnvc post about why crypto may be the most natural first environment for AI agents, based on the idea that agents need fast feedback loops and crypto provides a 24/7, permissionless, economically measurable loop.

## Decisions

- Use `The First Infinity Loop` as the title to introduce a new concept rather than only describe the AI + crypto trend
- Keep the post in `category: "blog"` with a technical opinion style, not dvvv
- Make the thesis explicit near the beginning: crypto may not be the first industry to apply AI, but it may be the first environment designed well enough for AI to operate on its own
- Use feedback loop as the central mechanism: observe, decide, act, measure, learn
- Replace long industry-by-industry prose with a compact table comparing feedback speed across software, marketing, legal, and trading
- Emphasize 24/7 operation, declining agent cost, continuous audit, and permissionless execution as the four supporting pillars
- End on the stronger claim that crypto may be where the economy of AI is born
