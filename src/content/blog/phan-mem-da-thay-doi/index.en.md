Hey, it's him again, a street scientist

After being away from the job market for some time, returning now, he feels clearly that the job of writing software has changed completely

All day, he chats with ChatGPT and Windsurf. Learning new things, nurturing ideas, then selecting what fits his current needs and future direction. Once a concrete plan is in place, he lets Windsurf realize it for him

AI helps him become a better programmer by offering multiple objective perspectives, explaining things he does not yet understand, and handling simple tasks so he can focus on what truly matters. AI, to him now, is like a capable assistant

Like it or not, we are standing before a massive transition, following the flow of cosmic energy. According to Xuan Kong Fei Xing, a discipline he recently stumbled upon, we are officially entering a new era: Period 9, the Fire Period

Where the fire of wisdom will burn away and expose all false egos. What remains wavering, hesitant, and unready for transformation will be discarded. Period 9 is the time for creativity, speed, intuition, and communication: values that demand people live mindfully and commit fully

Looking back at the previous three periods, they align perfectly with the formation and development of Vietnam:

- Period 6 (1964 to 1983) was the Metal era, a time of military strength, with the Northwest region benefiting the most
- Period 7 (1984 to 2003) was the time of communication and finance
- Period 8 (2004 to 2023) was the Earth era, a time when real estate reigned supreme

From Period 9 (2024 to 2043), Southern Vietnam, especially Saigon, will be the land receiving the new power of the universe

He believes 2025 and 2026 will be two pivotal years shaping a new order: when technology, creativity, spirituality, and communication rise together to rule the era. And he chooses to live in this period with mindfulness

A very good quote from the book Dao Trading he is reading:

"Mindfulness is not a state, but a return: immediately, right here"
