Hey, it's him again: today, Her cooked oyster porridge for him, giving him enough energy to reorganize his chaotic pile of knowledge

Aside from the porridge cooked by Her, he is very selective about all the foods, good or bad, in this universe before taking them into his body. For he knows that he is what he eats

Knowledge is the same: he often jokes that knowledge has now become cheap. This only makes him more selective before consuming any information. He views the consumption of knowledge just like his daily meals

It happens regularly and continuously. Knowledge is not only found in fine dining restaurants or from luxury chefs, but also all around him, on the way to the office, or at the breakfast stalls where the vendor serves a different dish each day and he often visits

So he is never in a rush, never frantic in absorbing knowledge. He does not care if this knowledge is tastier than that, or if this chef is better than another. He simply views this as a natural process, a basic need of life, an elegant form of entertainment

He always tries to eat lightly, just enough each day. This allows him to digest, and lets the food transform into positive energy for him

Even so, the porridge cooked by Her is never enough for him
