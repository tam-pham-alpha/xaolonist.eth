Hey, it's him again, Brother Four Professor: who knows, a missed date might cost a hundred years of waiting. Is it true that the universe will always send us lessons until we finally pass the test? How many times must his shallow self be battered by the universe before gaining some depth?

This Lunar New Year, he was busy cleaning the house and sorting through his chaotic thoughts. Without paying attention, he reached 69kg before he knew it. The favorite pair of pants he often wore had grown noticeably tight. It could be said that reaching 69kg was his greatest measurable achievement in the past year of 2024

Previously, Her told him about energy. However, only recently did he pay close attention, as Einstein once said: 'Everything is energy and that's all there is to it. Match the frequency of the reality you want and you cannot help but get that reality. It can be no other way. This is not philosophy. This is physics.'

He likes the number 69, for it symbolizes rhythmic and eternal vibration. He hopes that reaching 69kg will help him align with the positive frequency of the universe, helping him connect with himself, nature, and resonate with and amplify the energy of the people he loves
