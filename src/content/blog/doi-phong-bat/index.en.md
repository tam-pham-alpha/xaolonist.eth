Hey, it's him again: lighting an herbal stick, popping open a Hoegaarden to continue writing, to continue living a flamboyant life of contemplations

If you have never intended to end your life, you are fortunate. If that thought once crossed your mind but you let it go, and now you are full of hope for a good future, you are blessed. If someone gave you timely advice to pull back from that action, you have accumulated great merit. But many others are not so fortunate. He has seen it

And he is a man of fortune. He knows that almost everything in this world, unless it involves life and death, is a minor matter. He once spent all his savings to experience life. Afterwards, what he received was much greater than what he had given away. He once bought the bottom of LUNA with all the money he had, like many others, losing it all. But then LUNC returned everything he had lost, even shaking and tossing it, overflowing, in abundance

The money in his pocket comes and goes. But the knowledge, experience, and wisdom he accumulates grow thicker every day. Last Lunar New Year, he drew this blessing: "Therefore do not worry, saying, 'What shall we eat?' or 'What shall we drink?' or 'What shall we wear?' For your heavenly Father knows that you need all these things." So if anyone out there felt less than lucky last year, take a deep breath and keep pushing: you will get used to it slowly

If you are nearby, stop by: he will share some herbs
