Hey, it's him again. Today he will speak of life, just waking up to so many invitations.

And today he will speak of life<br/>
Just woke up and so many invitations arrive<br/>
From KMS, Zalo, and Axon too<br/>
But currently he has no wish to move on anew

Knowing that working anywhere is much the same<br/>
If you work hard, life won't leave you in pain<br/>
Ups and downs in life, who can foresee<br/>
No matter what, this universe spins endlessly

And what about your love life, how does it go<br/>
Are you going to run through the whole universe so<br/>
Even if your hands reach the stars in the night<br/>
When the sun rises, does your heart feel light?

Life goes high as an elephant, low as a dog<br/>
Live so your brothers are there through the fog<br/>
Sometimes all you need is a pot of braised pork<br/>
To warm your heart through the storms on your walk
