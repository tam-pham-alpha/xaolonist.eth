Hey, it's him again, and he is beautiful. To him, beauty is a superpower, and simply by dressing well, one is already halfway to success

Cinderella is a vivid testament. The job she was best at might have been distinguishing beans from sesame seeds. If the stepmother had mixed up True/False/Not Given, she might have missed her date with the prince. Yet, when she went out, she stepped out in a beautiful dress and a well-fitting pair of shoes

Women are born princesses, but only when they know how to drape themselves in a beautiful dress. Today, we do not need a fairy godmother: we can walk straight into Zara and select a perfectly fitting dress. Then, luck and excellence will automatically resonate with that beauty
