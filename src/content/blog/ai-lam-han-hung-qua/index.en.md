Hey hey, it's him again, and AI gets him too excited. He often jokes that before AI, only Tran Thanh had the capability to entertain: since AI arrived, even the vegetable vendor can create entertainment

Similarly, before AI, only large corporations could build good products and create a massive impact. Now, the gap between corporation and individual is no longer that wide

The near future will see an explosion of individuals, or small, lean, highly adaptable teams: as a result, many wonderful and not-so-wonderful things will happen together

A place where academic knowledge and cheap information might be treated equally will present a significant challenge to teenagers

He believes opportunities are now fairer for everyone. However, he knows that if AI has not yet saved him time, helped generate passive income, or given him access to a higher dimension, he is merely holding a giant toy
