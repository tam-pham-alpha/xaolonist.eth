Hey, it's him again: some call him Brother Four, others say Uncle Four. During his morning coffee, he reflects on life

After leaving Vu Dai village, he spent over 12 years in magnificent Saigon. He tasted all kinds of delicacies and experienced a few bitter fruits. Life in Saigon gave him almost everything he needed, yet he always felt unsatisfied. Was he too greedy?

He has been back in Kon Tum for over 2.5 years now. Here, it is hard to find beautiful, sophisticated things like in Saigon, but there is no shortage of interesting dishes: Phu Tho pork platter, Son Goat hotpot: also known as Goat Son: Shop 65 on Duy Tan with premium beef tendon, Ly Ngu Duck, and plenty of other cool things in the world. For hidden treasures, you only need to lift the rustic leaf to see the good things

Kon Tum offers him a chance to escape the hamster wheel of fame and wealth. He has time to go inward, to discover what he truly needs. The time here has not been long, yet he believes the things he learned outnumber his entire previous life combined. He connected with the people and nature here: even though the air is dry and hot as hell, he thought to himself

He remembers Her, who loved, cared for, and cherished him: who stood by him when he was weakest and most vulnerable. To him, Her always holds a key, indelible place in his heart. People only know Her for Her chapped lips: but they do not know she has a beautiful soul, a warm heart, and a very round, juicy bust. He measured it: it was 4.5 spans
