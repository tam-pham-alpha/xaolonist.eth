Hey, it's him again, and Mrs. Tien's 13k Stone-Grilled Sausage has surpassed many other dishes to claim the top spot in his rankings

Previously, when living a flamboyant life in Saigon, he frequently lost himself at Pizza 4P's, famous for its Burrata cheese made entirely in Vietnam. Their pizzas are handmade and offer various combinations of East and West. On weekends, he often frequented Soma, a small cafe in Thao Dien where flowers were always on the tables. The amiable French owner always reserved a corner for him and treated him with care whenever he visited. Green on Toast was the perfect choice for weekend brunch

Out of a seven-day week, he spent eight days at Pasteur Street, a craft brewery inspired by major Vietnamese fruits like dragon fruit and passion fruit: the food there was also light and suited his taste. In addition, Heart of Darkness, East West, and 7 Bridges were choices he highly valued

Occasionally he also stopped by Marou to enjoy the world's best chocolate. It is no exaggeration to say that Vietnam has the necessary and sufficient conditions to produce premium foodstuffs. He loves things with aroma and taste. Furthermore, behind every brand are interesting stories written in Vietnam, completed with the significant contributions of Vietnamese people

Now, he temporarily sets aside his former flamboyant lifestyle. In the afternoon, at 4:20pm, after a tiring day of work, he rewards himself with a walk along the embankment and a 13k Stone-Grilled Sausage from Mrs. Tien. Every time he arrives, without a word, Mrs. Tien hands him a sausage: a habit formed over a decade. His life is plain and highly predictable

The sausage is neither too big nor too long, fitting the mouth perfectly. Although one could cram the whole sausage inside, he advises young girls not to do so, nor to suck on it greedily. Because the sausage is very hot and spicy, and it is not ice cream. Girls should only take small bites: both showing good breeding and fully tasting its flavor. He loves the taste of the sausage: spicy at first, yet leaving a cool, lingering aftertaste

He is happy with his current life. He realizes that his value and health do not become less appealing or attractive just because of simple things
