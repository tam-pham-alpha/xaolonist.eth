Hey, it's him again, and lately he's been busy buying more time for himself

He does not know how time is measured in quantum mechanics, or how space-time is warped in Einstein's theory of relativity. But in his frame of reference, time is a priceless asset, and he has never regretted continuously buying more time for himself

An air fryer, a Dreame robot vacuum cleaner like a fairy stepping out of a folktale, helping him have more time to enjoy his coffee every morning: a cup with a handle

On a larger scale, it is using his own money to buy other people's time. And a revolution is when one uses ideals to make others voluntarily dedicate their precious time

Yet, even now, he still cannot understand what makes two strangers, sharing no blood relation, bond with each other and spend most of their lives together. It goes beyond the boundaries of money and ideals: it is a sacredness that his vocabulary cannot possibly describe. The energy from this connection could be strong enough to light up heaven or burn down hell
