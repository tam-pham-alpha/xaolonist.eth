Hey, it's him again, and last night Son Tung M-TP released Come My Way, and perhaps Vietnam owes him a deep thank you

If this song succeeds internationally, it could become a massive gateway for Vietnamese culture to step onto the global stage. When music leads the way, tourism, cuisine, fashion, and many other values of the country will also have the opportunity to be seen more

The most interesting part is that this could become a beautiful example of combining East and West while preserving a unique identity. Once that door is opened, opportunities for other Vietnamese artists to enter the international market will also become wider and easier

Tung is not just making music: he is trying to paint a new trend, a new direction for Vietnamese music. And whatever the final result may be, just having the courage to try is already worth appreciating

Thank you Tung, and ❤ you
