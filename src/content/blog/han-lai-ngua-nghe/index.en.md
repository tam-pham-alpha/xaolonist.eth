Hey, it's him again: Tu, a scientist. He doesn't remember the last time he wrote futile things, but today he suddenly itches to write again

He often jokes with himself that in the future, if he no longer writes code, he will become a writer to write about his life, the people he met, and the roads he traveled

Lately, he has been working out quite regularly, which has given him a strong physique: broad chest, toned glutes, and a firm abdomen. The target is 70 kilograms, with only 6.9 kg left to go

He always tells himself that the abdomen is the most important part of the body: a strong core is necessary to bear the weight of the upper half and serves as the bridge for the body to perform complex, high-paced movements. Therefore, he prioritizes core workouts: training them more than other areas. Currently, his abs have 2 packs, and soon they will be 6 packs

Recently, he has managed his emotions better: he no longer holds grudges or hates anyone. Probably because lately he has been listening to self-cultivation teachings. He doesn't know if he will reach enlightenment, but life has become slightly gentler with him

He lives a leisurely life: morning coffee at 6:50am, and returning to his desk at 8:10am. He works, eats, and rests reasonably, or at least he thinks so. He often drinks orange juice in the morning, which keeps him healthy. Occasionally he still eats onion porridge, but this time it is because he wants to

He lives, works, and makes money to realize his ambition: to step honorably on the path of socialism. He does not know if the road is far and long, but he simply walks, without a shred of doubt or calculation
