Hey, it's him again. He is binge-watching Demi-Gods and Semi-Devils: the very same series he watched voraciously on VHS tapes back in 7th grade, now watching on YouTube

He has washed his hands in a golden basin and chosen the righteous path: he is no longer a xa-o-lo-nist, but anh4gs, pronounced as Brother Four Professor, marking his transition to focus his full energy on satirical science research

There are old movies and old books: but depending on the timing, they bring him entirely different lessons

In the past, he wanted to possess the ultimate martial arts under heaven: however, he now understands that if internal power is strong but not accompanied by great spiritual strength, one is highly likely to deviate into madness, with an unstable mind. He realizes that with a different mindset, sometimes we see new things in what seemed so familiar
