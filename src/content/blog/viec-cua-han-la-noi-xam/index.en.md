Hey, it's him again: his job is to talk nonsense. If you believe what he says, that's up to you

After talking back to the boss and having deep technical arguments with colleagues, he became a full-time blogger instead of a full-time programmer. He considers this a major turning point in his life: now, unemployment is a choice he can actively choose

He has lived long enough to know it is a great bargain to exchange anything he owns for a deep, dreamless sleep

In the past, Vu Trong Phung once wished to exchange his writings for beef. But humans prefer tangible things: especially things that can fill the stomach: rather than futile literature, which only serves to satisfy the souls of lustful dreamers

Though he knows writing is an ungrateful profession, if it cannot be exchanged for beef, could he at least exchange his prose for some Catsrang? He has many cats. Meow meow 🐱😹😻😿😸😽
