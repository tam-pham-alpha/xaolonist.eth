Hey hey, it's him again. Before, he thought that having as much knowledge as possible was best. But then, he realized that some knowledge eventually becomes outdated, or simply no longer fits

Just as heaven and earth have light and darkness, and trees photosynthesize then respire, humans are the same: learning and unlearning, seeing and unseen, loving and being loved, everything intertwines, inseparable

Those are the very basic rotations of the universe, where receiving the new, filtering, and discarding what is no longer suitable always go hand in hand, so that each person gradually becomes a stronger version of themselves, and more suited to the present: may he always remain The Fool
